About Top Shelf Hockey

At Top Shelf Hockey, we’re more than just a hockey shop — we’re a community built by players, for players. Founded in 2015, our goal has always been to provide top-quality gear, expert service, and a passion for the game that keeps skaters coming back. Whether you’re lacing up for your first shift or sharpening your skills for the big leagues, we’re here to keep you at the top of your game.

Our staff includes former players and certified equipment specialists who know the game inside and out. From custom skate fittings to the latest stick technology, we make sure every piece of equipment performs as hard as you do.
